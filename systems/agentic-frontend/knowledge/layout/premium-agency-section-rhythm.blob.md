Best-practice rules:
- Give hero and major proof sections more breathing room than utility sections.
- Prefer fewer stronger content groups over many shallow cards.
- Alternate dense proof sections with quieter sections to prevent monotony.
- Preserve a clear CTA path without turning every section into a CTA panel.
- Keep mobile spacing reduced but still premium.

Implementation pattern:
- Define section roles: hero, proof, service, work, testimonial, CTA.
- Assign spacing based on role importance.
- Keep content groups aligned to a shared container.
- Verify the next section is hinted when the hero occupies the first viewport.

Anti-patterns:
- Oversized hero followed by disconnected content.
- Repeated floating cards as the main visual language.
- Equal section spacing regardless of importance.
